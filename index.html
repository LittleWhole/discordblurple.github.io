WHAT IS A BLURPLE LOGO, AND HOW CAN I MAKE ONE?

I think they are trending right now...

A blurple logo is a logo that uses the two colours Blurple and White. The logo only uses these two colours, and generally is quite minimalistic, not with heaps of detail. The logo cannot have any other colours otherwise it will not pass the submission queue. The only exception is dark Blurple, which can be used as a shade of blurple, to make it easier to distinguish details.
Blurple - #7289DA or rgb(114, 137, 218)
White - #FFFFFF or rgb(255, 255, 255)
Dark Blurple - #4e5d94 or rgb(78, 93, 148)
